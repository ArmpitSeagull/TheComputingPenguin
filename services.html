Our repair service allows you to bring in any damaged computers and any computer peripherals that may need repaired. Our price will typically range from £25 to £250 depending on the extent of the damage to your device and the length it may take to repair your device and what may need repairing.

You may book a time, so you are confident that you are able to get a place and that it may fit your schedule.

Laptop Repair

Repairing a laptop will be on the latter side of repairs, whereas repairing a mouse or a keyboard for PC will be on the cheaper side, of course still depending on the damage of said item and the time it may take. The price may also vary above £250 range if the repair turns out to be a hardware problem, then a replacement will of course be needed along with the cost of said repair, the problem may be strictly Sofware, in which case replacement parts will not be needed for repair.
